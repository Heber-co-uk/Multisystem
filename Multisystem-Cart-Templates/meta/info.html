Multisystem Expansion Cartridge

A project template for Multisystem SNAC cartridges with:

  • Schematic sheet with edge connector and USB connector labelled with appropriate nets
  • PCB sheet with outline for a standard 72x50mm cartridge and appropriately-positioned edge connector, USB connector and holes

Also includes all of the following settings from the Heber template:

  • 2 layers (to change this, open the PCB editor, and to go File > Board Setup > Board Stackup > Physical Stackup)
  • Sheet borders configured
  • BOM and gerber export configured
  • Design rules set up to suit the standard processes of our manufacturers
  • Net classes including a Power net class for thicker tracks on power nets by default
